Current Address

Own or Rent

Choose the option that describes the current status of the borrower's physical address. Rent if currently paying rent to a landlord or Own if currently owning free and clear or making mortgage loan payments.

Enter the total number of years the borrower has lived at this address. If borrower has both rented and owned at this address, add the rented and owned years together.
