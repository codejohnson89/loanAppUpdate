Click 'Finish' to send your application.

If you're finished with your application and ready to send it to your loan officer, just click 'Finish'. You can also return later to complete your application by clicking on the 'Loan Application' link and selecting 'Finish loan application' under 'Returning Visitors'. Everything you've entered so far has already been saved.

Once your application is submitted, you'll receive e-mail notifications as each step of your loan process is completed. You can also log into your own private area of our web site to view the progress of your loan or attach other documents.

DirectFax

Our DirectFax technology allows you to securely attach paper documents to your loan application using any fax machine. Fax the provided cover page along with your documents to the toll free number printed on it. Or, if you already have your documents in an electronic format such as PDF, click here to upload them. Click 'Print Cover Page' and choose the printer to send it to.
